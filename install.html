Install Meal Planner App on Your iPhone

This app works offline and can be added to your iPhone’s home screen like a native app.

1. Open the app in Safari

Go to your-app-url (replace with your hosting URL) in Safari on your iPhone.

2. Tap the Share button

At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share icon
(a square with an arrow pointing up).

3. Choose “Add to Home Screen”

Scroll down the share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen.

4. Confirm the name and tap “Add”

The Meal Planner icon will now appear on your home screen. Tap it anytime to open the app offline.
